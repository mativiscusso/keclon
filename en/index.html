KECLON develops innovative enzymes as environmentally friendly solutions to improve the performance of industrial processes.

Our R&D team, large scale fermentation technology, and state-of-the-art downstream processing consolidate our position as regional leaders in the industrial supply of sustainable solutions.

out company

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

Our company, founded by Argentinian scientists, design, produce and commercialize enzymes, complying with the highest international quality standards.

We have developed a technological platform to rapidly generate new enzymes by using directed evolution, genetic engineering and synthetic biology tools. Our intellectual property portfolio reflects our commitment to innovation.

SUSTAINABILITY

The use of enzymes for large-scale industrial processes is the fundamental cornerstone of modern green chemistry technologies.

We contribute to industrial development without causing any environmental harm by replacing synthetic catalysts for biodegradable enzymes that reduce carbon dioxide emissions as well as water and energy consumption.

VALUE GENERATION

We offer solutions for food processing, animal nutrition, edible oil and biodiesel industries in Latin-America and other emerging markets.

Our enzymes improve the efficiency of industrial processes which maximizes our customers’ investment return.

INVESTMENT IN TECHNOLOGY

We are building our first large-scale fermentation plant for production of enzymes equipped with the latest technology, ensuring quality and costs necessary to be leaders in the most demanding markets.

OUR PRODUCTS

edible oils

Zero Residue Biorefinery

Our enzymes can be combined in order to provide oil producers with comprehensive solutions by turning waste into value.


PLASEMIX™ ™ offers an alternative to the conventional aqueous degumming of crude vegetable oil, reaching yields greater than 2% w/w and with lower refining costs. Our phospholipases convert the phospholipids (PLs) into diacylglycerides. As the PLs are hydrolyzed, emulsions are no longer formed and therefore the retained oil is released. The gums obtained from the traditional aqueous degumming processes can also be treated, resulting in the recovery of extra oil.



OLEINZYME™ was developed to transform edible oil and food industry byproducts — such as gums, soapstocks, or combinations of both — by treating them with an exclusive enzymatic preparation which greatly reduces its environmental impact and has superior characteristics as opposed to those of the traditional methods which rely on strong acids. This enzymatically-obtained acid oil can be employed as an additive to animal feed, among other uses.

biodiesel

SGASE™ Our novel hydrolase completely removes steryl glycosides preventing the formation of insoluble precipitates, which account for major operational issues in the production of biodiesel and compromise the quality of the final product.


Our low-cost and high-efficiency biodiesel treatment minimizes the total amount of contaminants and enhances cold filtration properties and visual appearance (haze).


This enzymatic solution is compatible with the most common biodiesel production systems, such as Lurgi, Desmet Ballestra, and others, and can be introduced prior to the final drying step.
